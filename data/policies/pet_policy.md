# PORTICO PET POLICY

## OVERVIEW
Portico communities welcome responsible pet owners. Pets are allowed with prior written approval and subject to restrictions outlined below.

## PET APPROVAL PROCESS

1. Submit Pet Approval Form (available at leasing office) with photos
2. Pet must be up-to-date on vaccinations (veterinary proof required)
3. No more than 2 pets per unit (combination of cats and dogs)
4. Management reserves right to deny approval for aggressive breeds

## PROHIBITED BREEDS

The following breeds are NOT permitted:
- Pit Bull (any mix)
- Rottweiler
- German Shepherd (except certified service animals)
- Chow Chow
- Akita
- Husky/Malamute mixes
- Any dog with history of aggression or bites

## PET FEES & DEPOSITS

- Pet deposit: $300 per pet (refundable, non-refundable if damage occurs)
- Pet rent: $35/month per pet
- Pet fee at lease signing: $100 per pet (non-refundable)

## PET RESTRICTIONS

- Pets must be contained within unit at all times
- Common area leash laws: All dogs must be on-leash in common areas
- Noise restrictions: Excessive barking (more than 10 minutes continuous) is lease violation
- No exotic pets, reptiles, or birds

## RESIDENT RESPONSIBILITIES

- Maintain current vaccinations (proof required annually)
- Responsible for all damages caused by pet (beyond normal wear)
- Must clean up all waste in common areas immediately
- Liability insurance may be required (landlord's option)

## SERVICE ANIMALS & EMOTIONAL SUPPORT ANIMALS

Certified service animals are exempt from pet fees and breed restrictions. Emotional support animals require documentation from licensed mental health professional and are subject to pet fees.

## VIOLATIONS & ENFORCEMENT

First violation: Written warning and 30-day cure period
Second violation: Non-renewal or 30-day lease termination notice
Dangerous pet incidents: Immediate removal required
