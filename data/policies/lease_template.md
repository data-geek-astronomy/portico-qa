# PORTICO STANDARD LEASE AGREEMENT

## 1. LEASE TERM
This Lease is entered into for a Term of twelve (12) months, commencing on the 1st day of the month and ending on the last day of the twelfth month, unless extended or terminated earlier pursuant to the terms hereof.

Early Termination: Resident may terminate this lease with 60 days written notice and payment of lease break fee equal to 1.5 months' rent, provided that rent is current and unit is in good condition.

## 2. RENT PAYMENT

Monthly rent is $1,500 due on the 1st of each month. A late fee of 5% of monthly rent applies if payment is not received by the 5th of the month. Three (3) late payments within a 12-month period may result in non-renewal or lease termination.

Rent increases: Annual lease renewals include a rent adjustment not to exceed 5% per community market rate guidelines.

## 3. SECURITY DEPOSIT

Security deposit of $1,500 (equal to one month's rent) is held as security for performance of lease obligations. Deposit is non-refundable for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

## 4. UTILITIES

Resident is responsible for electric, gas, water, sewer, trash, and cable services. All utility meters are individual. Utilities are NOT included in rent.

## 5. LEASE RENEWAL

Lease renewal notices must be provided 90 days before lease expiration. Renewal rent shall be established based on current market rates and resident's lease compliance history. Residents not in good standing will not be offered renewal.

"Good standing" means: no late payments in past 12 months, no lease violations, and no unresolved maintenance issues resulting from resident negligence.

## 6. MOVE-OUT REQUIREMENTS

Resident must provide 60 days written notice of non-renewal. Unit must be vacated by 11:59 PM on final lease date, thoroughly cleaned, and all keys returned. Failure to vacate results in holdover rent at 150% of monthly rent until unit is vacated.

## 7. SUBLETTING

Subletting is prohibited without written consent of Landlord. Unauthorized subletting is grounds for lease termination.

## 8. GOVERNING LAW

This lease is governed by the laws of the State in which the property is located.
