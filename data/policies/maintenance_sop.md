# MAINTENANCE STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

## REQUEST PROCESS

1. Resident submits maintenance request via tenant portal or phone: 1-800-PORTICO
2. Emergency requests (no heat, water, electrical hazard): 4-hour response
3. Non-emergency requests: 7-day response
4. Request logged with timestamp and priority level

## PRIORITY LEVELS

**EMERGENCY (0-4 hours)**
- No heat/AC (seasonal)
- No water supply
- Gas smell
- Electrical hazard
- Roof leak causing active dripping
- Broken door/window lock (safety)
- Sewage backup

**URGENT (1-2 days)**
- Appliance malfunction (oven, fridge, washer)
- Plumbing leaks (non-emergency)
- Broken plumbing fixtures
- Sliding door/window malfunction
- Pest infestation

**STANDARD (3-7 days)**
- Paint touch-ups
- Caulking/weatherstripping
- Door hinges/locks
- Cabinet repairs
- Light fixture replacement
- Flooring damage

## RESIDENT RESPONSIBILITIES

- Grant access to unit at scheduled time (24-hour notice required)
- Unit must be accessible (not locked, not blocked)
- Failure to grant access: request can be marked complete and re-submitted

## VENDOR SELECTION

Maintenance coordinator selects vendor based on:
- Specialty (plumbing, HVAC, electrical, general)
- Response time capability
- Cost per job
- Resident satisfaction scores (>4.2/5.0 stars)

Vendor performance tracked in quarterly reports.

## DOCUMENTATION

All maintenance work must include:
- Date/time of service
- Work description
- Materials used
- Labor hours
- Cost
- Resident signature
- Photos if damage assessment needed

## RECURRING MAINTENANCE

**Monthly:** HVAC filter replacement, pest control inspection
**Quarterly:** Fire extinguisher inspection, smoke detector testing
**Annually:** HVAC service, plumbing inspection, roof inspection

## LIABILITY & DAMAGE ASSESSMENT

- Damage from "normal wear and tear" = Portico covers
- Damage from resident negligence = Resident liable
- Intentional damage = Non-renewal + damage charges

Examples:
- Sink overflow from leaving faucet running = Resident liable
- Water heater failure after 10 years = Portico covers
- Broken window from throwing object = Resident liable
- Stained carpet from pet accident = Resident liable

## COST ALLOCATION

Maintenance costs <$100: Handled as routine
Maintenance costs $100-500: Approved by property manager
Maintenance costs >$500: Requires regional director approval
Estimated time to complete documented and communicated to resident within 24 hours.
