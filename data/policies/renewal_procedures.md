# LEASE RENEWAL PROCEDURES

## TIMELINE

**Day 120-90 before expiration:** Property manager reviews resident file
- Payment history
- Maintenance complaints
- Lease violations
- Move-in condition vs. current condition

**Day 90 before expiration:** Renewal decision made
- Good standing residents: Renewal offered
- Non-compliance residents: Non-renewal notice OR conditional renewal

**Day 90-60 before expiration:** Renewal offer communicated
- Renewal letter sent (email + certified mail)
- New lease terms and rent amount included
- Resident has 30 days to accept/decline

**Day 60 before expiration:** Final renewal deadline
- Acceptance deadline: Day 60 (30 days from offer)
- Unsigned leases do not guarantee occupancy
- New move-in scheduled if no acceptance

## RENEWAL CRITERIA

### Will NOT Renew:
- More than 2 late rent payments in past 12 months
- Any lease violation (unauthorized occupants, subletting, etc.)
- Outstanding balances owed
- Lease termination notice issued previously

### May Offer Conditional Renewal:
- 1 late payment in past 12 months (may require higher deposit)
- Minor maintenance damage (assessed repair cost >$200)
- Noise complaints (1-2) with pattern of improvement

### Will Renew at Standard Terms:
- No late payments
- No lease violations
- Good condition of unit
- Positive resident history

## RENEWAL RENT CALCULATION

Base rent increase calculation:
1. Review local market rent for comparable units
2. Apply annual escalation factor (2-5% per community policy)
3. Cap increases at annual limit (typically 5%)
4. Consider resident's lease compliance

Example:
- Current rent: $1,500
- Market rate for comparable: $1,575
- Escalation: 3%
- New rent: $1,545 (3% cap applied)

## NON-RENEWAL PROCESS

If renewal not offered:
1. Non-renewal notice issued 90 days before expiration
2. Move-out date is last day of lease term
3. Resident has 60 days to secure new housing
4. Final walkthrough scheduled
5. Deposit refund processed within 30 days

## MOVE-OUT INSPECTION

Within 5 business days after move-out:
- Unit inspected for damages beyond normal wear
- Damage photos taken
- Repair estimates obtained
- Deposit deductions documented

Deductions from deposit:
- Carpet cleaning/replacement
- Paint touch-up/full repaint
- Appliance repairs
- Fixture replacement
- Pest treatment
- Deep cleaning

Deposit refund mailed within 30 days with itemized deduction list.

## RENEWAL INCENTIVES

May offer conditional incentives to retain good residents:
- Rent concessions (reduced increase)
- Upgrade to larger unit at same rent
- Free unit upgrades (counters, flooring)
- Month free rent (if market indicates risk of non-renewal)

Requires regional director approval. Cannot discriminate by protected class.
